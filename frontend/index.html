How It Works
1
Paste any suspicious content — message, article, or job post
2
6 AI agents analyze it from different angles in sequence
3
Evidence from 18+ known scams is retrieved via semantic search
4
Critic agent challenges the analysis to reduce false positives
5
Verdict is explained in plain English with a confidence score
Red Flag Tips
🔴
OTP Requests
No legitimate bank or company will ever ask for your OTP.
🟠
Guaranteed Income
Jobs promising fixed daily earnings with no interview are always scams.
🟡
Urgency Language
"Act now", "expires in 2 hours" — pressure tactics signal manipulation.
🔵
Verify Sources
Breaking news without a verifiable outlet is likely misinformation.
